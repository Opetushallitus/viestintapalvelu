Congratulations!

You have been accepted to study in the above-mentioned programme in the spring 2026 joint application to upper secondary education and preparatory education.

Please remember to accept your study place no later than 25 June 2026. If you do not accept the place by the deadline, you will lose the study place reserved for you.

You will receive an email containing a link through which you can accept the study place. Alternatively, you may accept the study place by logging in to the My Studyinfo (Oma Opintopolku) service. Logging in requires strong authentication (e.g. online banking credentials). Please note that the educational institution may also require you to accept the study place in writing or by registering in person. Carefully read the instructions in the selection letter sent to you by the institution by post or email.

This letter also contains information about the status of your other application preferences in the student admission process. Application preferences ranked lower than the one to which you have been accepted have been automatically cancelled, and you can no longer be accepted to them. However, you may still be accepted from a waiting list to any application preferences ranked higher. Information about the admission criteria and scoring factors can be found in Studyinfo under Student admission in the joint application to upper secondary education and preparatory education.
