CONFIRMING THE STUDY PLACE AND REGISTRATION FOR STUDIES

Remember to confirm the study place and register as a student by the date given or you will lose the study place. (Universities Act 38 § and 43 § / Polytechnics Act 28c § 32 §). Confirm the study place preferably electronically with the link that has been sent to your email or in the My Studyinfo -service. After you have confirmed the study place, you can register as a student in the higher education institution either electronically (the link to the registration will open after you have confirmed the study place in the My Studyinfo -service) or in the way indicated by the higher education institution.

Confirming the study place

You must confirm the study place within 10 days after you have been accepted to the study place. Check the deadline at the bottom of the form for notification of confirmation for this study place. You can only confirm the study place once and you cannot change the confirmation you have made afterwards. You can only confirm one higher education study place starting in the same academic term (1 August to 31 December 2018). The one place per term provision applies to both joint application and separate applications in higher education (transfer student applications are not included in the provision).

You can confirm the study place electronically with the link that has been sent to your email. If you have Finnish online banking credentials, a mobile certificate or a smart ID card and a reader, you can confirm the place by logging in to the My Studyinfo -service from the link on top of the Studyinfo.fi front page. The My Studyinfo -service has instructions on how to confirm the study place electronically.

If you cannot confirm the study place electronically, confirm the study place by using the form on the next page. Make sure the form arrives to the address stated on the bottom of the page within 10 days after you have been accepted to the study place. If the form arrives to the higher education institution late, you will lose the study place.

When you confirm the study place, you will not be considered a first-time applicant in future applications. More information: Studyinfo.fi > Higher education > Applying > Quota for first-time applicants. After you have confirmed the study place, you must also register as an attending or a non-attending student for the academic year 2018-2019 by the date given by the higher education institution or you will lose your right to study.

Registering as a student

You can register as a student in the higher education institution either electronically through the national registration service when you confirm the study place or in another way indicated by the higher education institution.

If you confirm the study place but remain on the waiting list for a study place higher on your preference list, you can only register as an attending or a non-attending student after your study place has been confirmed by the higher education institution. More detailed instructions on how to register can be found from the registration service and the higher education institution you have applied to (see the link "Instructions for new students" at the bottom of the page).

NB! If you are required to pay tuition fees, you cannot register for the academic year before you have completed your payment. You can check this from the higher education institution.

You can register as non-attending for the first academic year only for statutory reasons, that is military or civilian service, maternity leave, paternity leave or parental leave or due to illness or disability. Not completing the registration will lead to forfeiting the right to study (Universities Act § 43 / Polytechnics Act § 32).
