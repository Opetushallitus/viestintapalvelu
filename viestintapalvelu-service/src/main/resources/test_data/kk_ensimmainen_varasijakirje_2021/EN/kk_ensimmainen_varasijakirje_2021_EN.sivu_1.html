Confirming a study place

You must confirm the study place mentioned above by $palautusTimestampEn or you will lose the study place (Universities Act 38 ยง and 43 ยง / Polytechnics Act 28c ยง 32 ยง). You can only confirm the study place once and you cannot change the confirmation you have made afterwards. You can only confirm one higher education study place starting in the same academic term (1st August to 31th December 2020). When you confirm a study place rest of the offered study places will be cancelled automatically. You cannot be accepted to other programmes starting in the same academic term after you have confirmed a study place. The one place per term provision applies to both joint application and separate applications in higher education (transfer student applications are not included in the provision). Confirm the study place preferably electronically with the link that has been sent to your email or in the My Studyinfo -service. If you have Finnish online banking credentials, a mobile certificate or a smart ID card and a reader, you can confirm the place by logging in to the My Studyinfo -service from the link on top of the Studyinfo.fi front page. The My Studyinfo -service has instructions on how to confirm the study place electronically.

If you cannot confirm the study place electronically, contact the higher education institution from which you want to confirm a study place. Make sure that the higher education institution receives your written confirmation no later than by $palautusTimestampEn. If the confirmation arrives to the higher education institution late, you will lose the study place.

When you confirm the study place, you will not be considered a first-time applicant in future applications.
