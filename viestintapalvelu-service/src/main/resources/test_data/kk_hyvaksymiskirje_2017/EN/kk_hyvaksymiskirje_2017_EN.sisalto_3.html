CONFIRMING THE STUDY PLACE AND REGISTRATION FOR STUDIES

Remember to confirm the study place and register as a student by the date given or you will lose the study place. Act 38 § and 43 § / Polytechnics Act 28c § 32 §). Confirm the study place preferably electronically in My Studyinfo -service. After you have confirmed the study place, you can register as a student in the higher education institution either electronically on the national registration service OILI (the link to OILI will open after you have confirmed the study place in the My Studyinfo -service) or in the way indicated by the higher education institution.

Confirming the study place

You must confirm the study place by Friday 15 July 2016 at 3 pm. You can only confirm one study place and you cannot change the confirmation you have made afterwards.

You can confirm the study place electronically by logging in to the My Studyinfo -service from the link on top of the Studyinfo.fi front page. In order to log in, you will need Finnish online banking credentials, a mobile certificate or a smart ID card granted by the police and a reader. My Studyinfo -service has instructions on how to confirm the study place electronically.

If you cannot confirm the study place electronically, confirm the study place by using this form. Make sure the form arrives to the address stated on the bottom of the page by Friday 15 July 2016 at 3 pm. If the form arrives to the higher education institution late, you will lose the study place.

When you confirm a study place, you will not be considered a first-time applicant in future applications. More information: https://studyinfo.fi/wp2/en/higher-education/quota-for-first-time-applicants/. After you have confirmed the study place, you must also register as an attending or a non-attending student for the academic year 2016-2017 by the date given by the higher education institution or you will lose your right to study.

Registering as a student

You can register as a student in the higher education institution either through the national registration service OILI when you confirm the study place or in another way indicated by the higher education institution.

If you confirm the study place but remain on the waiting list for a study place higher on your preference list, you can only register as an attending or a non-attending student after your study place has been confirmed by the higher education institution. More detailed instructions on how to register can be found from 1) OILI registration service 2) the higher education institution you have applied to (see the link “Instructions for new students” at the bottom of the page).

You can register as non-attending only for statutory reasons, that is military or civilian service, maternity leave, paternity leave or parental leave or due to illness or disability. Not completing the registration will lead to forfeiting the right to study (Universities Act § 43 / Polytechnics Act § 32).
