ACCEPTING A STUDY PLACE IN JOINT APPLICATION TO HIGHER EDUCATION IN AUTUMN 2014

You have been accepted in joint application to higher education in autumn 2014. To confirm your study place, you have to inform that you accept it. If you do not inform the acceptance by the deadline given in this letter, you will lose the offered study place.

You can accept the study place electronically on your own pages on Studyinfo.fi-service. The electronical acceptance of the study place requires registry to the service and strong authentication by using online bank service code or mobile authentication. If you cannot do the acceptance of the study place electronically, you can do it with returning the attached acceptance notification to the address given in it by deadline. In addition you need to enrol to your higher education institution as attending or not-attending student.

You can accept the study place offered to you either as binding or conditional. If you accept the study place as binding, other study options, to which you are on a waiting list or accepted, will be cancelled. If you accept the study place conditionally, you can still be on a waiting list to study options higher on your list. If you get accepted to a study programme higher on your list, the study place you accepted conditionally, will automatically be cancelled. You need to inform accepting a study place higher on your list separately. If you are not accepted to a study place higher on your list from a waiting list, the study place you accepted conditionally will change binding.
